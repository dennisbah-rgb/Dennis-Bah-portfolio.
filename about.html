About Me

My name is Dennis Bah, I’m 20 years old and was born in Stockholm, Sweden. I grew up with both of my parents and my younger brother, in a warm and supportive family. I have cultural roots from both Colombia and Guinea, which has given me a diverse background and an open-minded perspective on life.

Ever since I was a kid, I’ve always been passionate about sports — especially football. I started playing at a young age and later joined the AIK youth academy when I was 12 years old. Unfortunately, I had to stop playing after a knee injury, but that experience taught me discipline, teamwork, and perseverance.

During high school, I studied the Electrical and Energy program with a focus on computer systems. That’s where my interest in programming really began to grow. I quickly realized how creative and logical programming can be, and decided that I want to build my future around it.

As a person, I’m more of an introvert — calm and observant rather than outgoing. But I’m intelligent, quick to learn, and always punctual. I believe these qualities come from the way I was raised and my personal drive to constantly improve myself.
